Company Overview

Macen Wrapping and Packaging Corp. (MWPC) is a premier PEZA-registered, ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturer of high-grade polyethylene packaging solutions serving globally recognized multinational corporations across the Philippines. Operating with precision, reliability, and eco-consciousness, we seamlessly blend industrial capability with modern, sustainable practices.

Cavite Economic Zone (CEZ)

Strategically situated to feed the active manufacturing corridors of Cavite with uninterrupted high-performance materials output.

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • Enterprise PEZA Zone Operational Clearances
  • High-Speed Extrusion Lines

Lima Technology Center (LTC)

Located in Batangas, this flagship facility houses advanced production ecosystems integrated directly with large-scale conversion capabilities.

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Production Systems
  • Specialized Polyethylene Recycling Line
  • Sustainable On-site Waste Circularity Loop

Innovations & Developments

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Pioneering sustainable plastic processing layouts through active implementation of OXIO-BIO (Oxium additives) to induce targeted oxidation-biodegradation. Additionally manufacturing the specialized ECO PE Bag line using Ecoplas bio-based biodegradable resins to scale 100% degradable properties.

10-Micron Ultra-Thin Stretch Film

Engineering breakdown boundaries with lightweight architectural structures that maintain uncompromising structural integrity.

  • Exceptionally fluid manual/machine application
  • Dramatic raw material reduction & cost-efficiency
  • High tensile performance and structural recovery load retention

PE with Water-Based Ink Print

Transitioning graphic marking to ultra-low VOC profiles. Our custom configured printing setups ensure pristine visual results that remain completely resistant to moisture bleeding or surface breakdown.

  • Perfect for food-grade, high-end retail, and logistics assets
  • Completely custom sizes, clear scales, and high designs

Hybrid Extruder Machine (HEM-01)

Our strictly proprietary operational engineering breakthrough. The HEM-01 co-generates functional electricity dynamically harvested right from active machinery heat and kinetic loops to directly feed back into our office administrative infrastructure, knocking out grid dependency.
